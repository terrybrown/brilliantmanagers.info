How did you end up in management?

Most of us didn't plan it. We were good at something, so someone gave us a team. The skills that got us there — technical depth, individual output, focused execution — are mostly the wrong skills for the job we've just been given.

This guide is for people who want to change that. Not a list of tips. A framework for understanding what good management actually looks like — across six areas that matter.

Start wherever you are.
