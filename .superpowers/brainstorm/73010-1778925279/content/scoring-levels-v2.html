Scoring levels v2 — four named levels

"Unaware" removed. The guide provides context before the scorecard is used, so Developing is a sensible floor.

Four levels

1
Developing
You know this matters and you're actively working on it. The gap between knowing and doing is closing.
2
Practising
Applying with reasonable consistency. It's not automatic yet, but it's deliberate.
3
Proficient
This shows up reliably. The people around you notice and benefit from it.
4
Leading
You're role-modelling this and actively helping others develop it too.

Progression — how the guide and scorecard work together

Developing Building awareness through the guide
→
Practising Applying deliberately in day-to-day work
→
Proficient Consistent and noticed by others
→
Leading Teaching and role-modelling for the team
