More discoverable but risks feeling broken — a dimmed button invites clicking and then confusion.

Implementation either way: Homepage page.tsx wraps its content in <div className="dark">. Tailwind's dark: variants apply inside it regardless of the global next-themes state. Other pages read from ThemeProvider normally. Option A conditionally renders the toggle via a usePathname() check in the nav component.
