Scoring — named competency levels

Replacing 1–10 with five named levels. Each name carries its own meaning — no translation required.

Proposed levels (1 → 5)

1
Unaware
Not yet on your radar as something to develop or pay attention to.
2
Developing
Actively building awareness and beginning to apply this in practice.
3
Practising
Applying with reasonable consistency. Some rough edges, but deliberate.
4
Proficient
Reliably demonstrated. Others notice and benefit from this in you.
5
Leading
Role-modelling this for others. Helping people around you grow in this area.
